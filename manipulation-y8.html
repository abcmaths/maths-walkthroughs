2.1c · Substitution

Substitution into Expressions and Formulae

Foundation

Worked Example: Substitute x = −2 into 2x2 + 3x

Replace every x with (−2):   2(−2)2 + 3(−2)
Evaluate powers first: (−2)2 = 4   →   2×4 + 3×(−2)
= 8 − 6 = 2
Caution: Always use brackets when substituting negatives.   (−2)2 = +4, but −22 = −4.
