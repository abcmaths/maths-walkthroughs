4.1a · Averages & Spread

Mean, Median, Mode & Range

Foundation
Mean = sum of all values ÷ number of values
Median = middle value when sorted (average of two middle values for even n)
Mode = most frequent value  |  Range = largest − smallest

Worked Example: 12, 15, 9, 20, 14

Sum: 12 + 15 + 9 + 20 + 14 = 70. Mean = 70 ÷ 5 = 14
Sort: 9, 12, 14, 15, 20 → middle value (3rd of 5) = Median = 14
No value repeats → no mode. Range = 20 − 9 = 11
