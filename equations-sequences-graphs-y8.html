2.2 · Equations & Inequalities

Solving Linear Equations

FoundationExtended

Example A: Two-step  →  5x − 7 = 18

Add 7 to both sides:   5x = 25
Divide both sides by 5:   x = 5

Example B: Unknowns on both sides  →  3x + 4 = 2x + 11

Subtract 2x from both sides:   x + 4 = 11
Subtract 4 from both sides:   x = 7

Example C: Simple inequality  →  4x − 3 > 9

Add 3:   4x > 12
Divide by 4:   x > 3
Inequality flip rule: If you multiply or divide both sides by a negative number, flip the inequality sign.

2.4 · Graphs & Functions

Linear Graphs

FoundationExtended

Worked Example: Plot y = 2x − 3

Finding gradient from two points: (1, 2) and (5, 10)

Gradient = rise ÷ run = (y₂ − y₁) ÷ (x₂ − x₁)
= (10 − 2) ÷ (5 − 1) = 8 ÷ 4 = 2
